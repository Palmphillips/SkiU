Introducing...

Finally, a solution for skiiers and snowboarders of all levels at the University of Colorado-Boulder. Quicker, cheaper, and cleaner carpooling is here. SkiU is a carpooling app, similar to Uber, designed with CU students in mind. Simply login with your CU-Boulder email and discover drivers and passengers who are ready to hit the slopes. Use SkiU to find other CU students on their way to popular resorts such as Eldora, Breckenridge, and Winter Park. In addition, SkiU helps youind other CU students of similar skiing level and ride together.

How does it work?

After logging into SkiU, you are able to set up a profile with your skiing information: where you like to ski/ride, your skiing level, type of terrarin you like to ski, and a little blurb about yourself. Then, select days you want to go skiing and see a list of all the different drivers headed out that day. (Alternatively as a driver, you can create an event of when you want to head out and display it to prospective passengers.) Finally, sign up for a driver, agree on a location, and go catch some fresh pow!
